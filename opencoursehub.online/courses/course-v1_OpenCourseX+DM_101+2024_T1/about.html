About This Course

The Google Digital Marketing & E-commerce Certificate is a hands-on program developed by Google employees in the field to provide job-ready skills in under 6 months. It covers key topics like email marketing, designing and running an online store, social media marketing, and marketing analytics. The curriculum consists of 7 courses, teaching learners how to use popular tools like Shopify, Mailchimp, Twitter, Google Ads, Google Analytics, and more.

This comprehensive program is designed to provide learners with a solid foundation in digital marketing and e-commerce, making it an ideal choice for those looking to develop their skills in these areas. No prior experience is required to enroll, and the certificate is endorsed by the American Association of Advertising Agencies (4A's) and the American Advertising Federation (AAF).

This certificate is designed for anyone who wants to learn digital marketing to build an online presence and engage customers online. After completing the program, graduates will have access to career resources and be connected with employers hiring for entry-level digital marketing and e-commerce roles.

Requirements

No prior experience is required to enroll in this course. It is designed for anyone who wants to learn digital marketing and e-commerce.
